FIRNI (AFGHAN CUSTARD)

Firni - a light, easy-to-make custard with flavours of cardamom, pistachios and almonds.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups whole milk
  • 5 tbs cornstarch
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • ½ tsp freshly ground cardamom
  • 1 tsp rosewater (optional)
  • A handful of chopped almonds and pistachio

    • Directions

    1. Pour 1 cup of milk into a small bowl, add the cornstarch and whisk them well until smooth.
    2. Pour the rest of the milk into a non-stick saucepan, adding the sugar and cardamom (and rosewater, ifdesired) whilst cooking over medium heat.
    3. Before it starts to boil, slowly add the milk and cornstarch mixture to the pan, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Keep stirring until the mixture increases in thickness.
    4. Take the pan off the heat and pour into a large shallow serving bowl. Let it cool at room temperature, andthenput in the refrigerator for about 1 – 2 hours.
    5. Finally, sprinkle your Firni with sliced almonds and pistachios and serve!
